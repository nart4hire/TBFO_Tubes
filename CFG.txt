Itters -> Var | Num | Str | Tuple
Dats -> Var | Num | Str | Tuple | List | Dict | Method | Dats Dot Method
Args -> Dats Comma Args | Dats
Tuple -> Pareno Parenc | Pareno Args Parenc
List -> Sqbo Sqbc | Sqbo Args Sqbc
Dict -> Curlyo Curlyc | Curlyo Itters Colon Dats Curlyc
Method -> Var Tuple | Method Dot Method
Compare -> Var Parator Compare | Boolean Parator Compare | Num Parator Compare | Var | Boolean | Num
Boolean -> "True" | "False"
Parator -> "<" | ">" | Asgn Asgn | Legr Asgn
Legr -> "<" | ">"
